Greenhouse: 3D Modeling

Greenhouse Project is 3d modeling project that showcases the awareness of the climate change around the world.The "Greenhouse Project" is a visionary 3D modeling initiative designed to highlight the global impact of climate change through vivid and interactive visualizations. This project employs cutting-edge 3D technology to create a series of virtual greenhouses, each representing different climate zones around the world. Inside these virtual environments, users can observe the effects of climate change on various ecosystems, from melting polar ice caps to desertification in arid regions. The project aims to educate and engage the public by demonstrating the fragility of our environment and the urgent need for climate action. Interactive features allow users to see how changes in variables like temperature and carbon dioxide levels could impact these delicate ecosystems in the future.
